Where to Find Us

From the slopes of Parnassus to the whispering Aegean, our tea journeys through Greece with the same reverence as the myths that shaped it. Visit our sanctuaries below— small, sun-lit spaces where aroma, craft, and story converge.

Athens – Plaka Atelier

Tucked beneath the Acropolis, our flagship shop welcomes wanderers with hand-blended brews, olive-wood tins, and tasting rituals guided by our herbalists.

Open daily · 10:00–20:00

Thessaloniki – Seafront Nook

Along the Thermaic Gulf, our northern haven hums with spice-laced breezes and bold blends inspired by the city’s crossroads of cultures.

Tue–Sun · 11:00–19:00

Delphi – Mountain Workshop

The heart of our craft. Here, on Parnassus’ flank, leaves are sorted, dried, and whispered into their final forms. Tours by appointment only.

Guided visits · Request online

Santorini – Sunset Kiosk

Perched above the caldera, this intimate kiosk offers chilled herbal infusions and rare volcanic blends at golden hour.

Seasonal · April–October
